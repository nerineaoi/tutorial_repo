About Our Team

We are a dedicated group of gaming enthusiasts committed to providing the best gaming experience. Our team consists of professional players, developers, and support staff who work tirelessly to ensure our community thrives.


Founded in 2010, we have grown from a small group of friends to a large community with members from all around the world. Our mission is to create a welcoming environment where gamers can connect, compete, and collaborate.


We believe in the power of gaming to bring people together and foster friendships. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive gamer, there's a place for you in our community.


Our Values

  • Community:
    We prioritize building a strong, inclusive community where everyone feels welcome.
  • Integrity:
    We uphold the highest standards of honesty and fairness in all our interactions.
  • Excellence:
    We strive for excellence in everything we do, from gameplay to customer support.
  • Innovation:
